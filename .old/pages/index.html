Welcome to the GBC All-in-One Advanced Building Platform

The purpose of this application is to provide an offline-first, one-stop shop for builders across North America. Our goal is to provide the most up-to-date guides, technical data and installation techniques on various materials such as Huber Zip System, Siga and more.

Each material page will include an interactive 3D model that you can view directly on your phone and accompanying documentation, both from the manufacturer and industry professionals covering best practices, installation methods, and more. The app will be fully functional offline, with the ability to download and save models, documents and even videos directly to your device for offline viewing — handy for on-site locations without reliable internet access. The app will be fully open source, funded by manufacturers’ donations and supplemental purchasable content.
